Academy Prepares

 

How are we preparing your student for college and life?

Tomorrow’s Leaders

Through Academics, athletics, community service, outdoor education and extracurricular activities, Chargers have ample opportunities to become leaders.

Making a Difference

Academy students in every grade participate in Community Service. In 2017, Chargers in grades 8-12 logged more than 11,000 hours of community service.

Strong Bonds that Last

During their time at Academy, students build extraordinary relationships with classmates and faculty members – relationships that last a lifetime.

Nationally Ranked

In 2017, 25 Academy juniors were named National Merit Scholarship Semifinalists, putting us among the top 10 independent schools in the nation for the tenth consecutive year.

The Class of 2018 is attending 94 colleges in 34 states, Germany, and Canada.
